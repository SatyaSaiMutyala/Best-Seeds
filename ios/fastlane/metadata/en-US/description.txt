Drive Bestseed is a professional delivery management platform built for the aquaculture seed logistics industry. Whether you're a driver delivering seeds or a manager overseeing operations, this app keeps your entire delivery workflow running smoothly in real time.

KEY FEATURES

Real-Time GPS Vehicle Tracking
Track delivery vehicles live on an interactive map with real-time location updates, route visualization, and deviation alerts. Managers can monitor all active deliveries from a single dashboard.

Smart Booking Management
Create and manage bookings for hatchery deliveries, spot orders, and vehicle availability. Filter by status, search by booking ID or location, and track every order from creation to completion.

Multi-Stage Delivery Workflow
Follow each delivery through a clear 6-stage pipeline: Pending, Confirmed, Driver Assigned, In Journey, Delivered, or Failed. Every status change is tracked and visible to all stakeholders.

Driver & Employee Dashboards
Drivers see their assigned bookings, active routes, and delivery history. Managers get a comprehensive overview with advanced filtering, booking creation, alert monitoring, and driver tracking.

Route Visualization & Navigation
View pickup, drop-off, and current vehicle positions on Google Maps with route polylines. The app detects route deviations and alerts managers automatically.

Background Location Tracking
Continuous GPS tracking runs reliably in the background during active deliveries, ensuring no location data is lost even when the app is minimized.

Push Notifications & Alerts
Instant notifications for new bookings, status updates, route deviations, and delivery completions powered by Firebase Cloud Messaging.

Delivery Proof & Failure Logging
Mark deliveries as completed or record failure reasons (delays, wrong quantity, quality issues) for full accountability and reporting.

Multi-Drop Route Support
Handle complex delivery routes with multiple drop-off points. Track each drop individually with customer details, quantities, and completion status.

Secure Authentication
OTP-based mobile login ensures only authorized drivers and employees can access the platform.

Offline Capability
Local data caching means the app works reliably even in areas with poor connectivity. Data syncs automatically when connection is restored.

Built-in Support
Get help directly through WhatsApp or phone call integration within the app.
